# Privacy Policy (Draft)
This Privacy Policy explains how Linkr NZ Ltd collects, uses, discloses, and retains personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.
- Data Retention: Records may be retained up to 7 years or per user request for deletion.
- Consent: Key actions are captured in the ConsentRecord table with version and IP.
- Access & Correction: Users may request access/corrections via support@linkr.co.nz.
